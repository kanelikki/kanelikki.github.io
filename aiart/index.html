Many artists don't like AI drawing, and don't call it as art. Part of artists have fear about AI taking their job.

Blame the advertisements, all they say is "AI is magic", but magic is heresy. Don't fall into them.

And yes I don't like the stupid marketing.

But why are you upset?

They steal art style.

  • First, they need a lot of training model for stealing art. Second, the model should know what is your art style. You have to be famous, and very successful artist first.
    If you are mad on the famous artists' side, they are famous enough, so will be paid for proper art anyway.
  • Human also can copy, or being affected by others' art style. AI also learns like human, but they learn really fast, and in different way.
  • Their style has limit, and they may not give exactly what you want. This will be discussed later.
  • Lastly, artists are human, they cannot live forever, thus cannot draw forever.

AI still throws goofy results often. People do trial and error, add negative prompt word again and again to produce the final result.

Yes, there are tons of abandon odd result behind some good shots. You need luck.

Will AI take the artist job?

Yes and no.

Historically, photograph affected art style - people drew less realistic art.

But in the end, artists changed their art style.

At this point, you may think, 'but AI can make anything', but not really.

AI has limitations, so you can hit the AI's weak spot, or use AI as compliment tool, and you will be okay.

Face and learn about AI

AI reads and makes image very differently from human.

They use noise profile for understanding and generating the image.

Human cannot catch that noise - even the small noise change can break AI image recognition.

If this is still complicated, just remember their basic learning style is different from human.

The AI can catch the whole style, but their weak point is detail (and good negative prompts may or may not fix the issue).

So what is the strength and limitation of the AI?

AI is good with photorealistic images, close-up images of one object, and landscapes.

Currently they aren't that good with anime details, but not bad either if you don't care of the details.

Then, what kind of art job AI cannot take (currently)?

Think of some images that are bad as JPEG format.

This says, if you are vector, pixel or UI artist, you are safe (at least now).

Also, AI is bad with making creative infamous character (I mean, like Patapon), since the source to learn is not enough for AI.

If you make creative character concept, don't worry about the AI.

(This doesn't count human-like character, unfortunately. Only unique shaped characters are counted.)

Here is another suggestion. Don't limit art to only result of drawing. You can share the amazing progreess of drawing.

Last but not least, have good communication skill with human.

Really, in real business, clients want to review and fix detail - tools are growing, so does request, that always happened.

If you can understand client request a lot better than AI, the client will have good reason to chose you instead of the AI.

How about using AI as a tool?

The one way, but artists don't like, is drawing or editing on AI generated drawing.

This saves time to draw, so it is effective way, but unfortunately, you may not claim copyright, since it is considered as AI work.

Then, how about this?

AI can help with your inspiration.

For example, imagine a car after 100 years, and you have no idea about that. AI can suggest how does it look like.

You can draw based on the 'hint' of the AI.

AI always generates image, even in the scenario that human cannot answer.

Society is not ready for this right now.

Since major artists are against AI drawing, like you, using AI as tool is unaccepted.

Furthermore, companies don't respect artists' work based on AI, because AI is trending. "AI made it" promotion works better than mentioning artists behind.

OK so? AI drawing isn't an art.

Considering people also learn from people, AI is a just an artist who is genius with learning.

Of course people ask to copy artists' style, and this may not considered as art.

But people usually mix more than one art styles to prompt. The result style can be unique.

If you tell to copy one artwork and nothing else, it isn't art.

If you tell to combinate styles, and add your imagination to prompt, it is whole new work, thus it is art.

Also, for input prompt, considering creative writing, it is art.

Conclusion

See, AI is not that as good as you think. But don't stay on your place. They are growing fast.

Historically, machine and IT replaced human's part of work, and as a result, the whole job structure has been changed.

It's not only artist, AI is changing the whole job market.

But one thing is sure, art is about creativity, after all. Find your strength that AI cannot follow.

Embrace AI's difference, learn different style, and make your own way. They just exist, and there's no point to be angry towards them.

AI images will be different genre of 'art'. Denying them will be gatekeeping eventually.

Also remember, companies exaggerate AI because it is marketing that makes money.
